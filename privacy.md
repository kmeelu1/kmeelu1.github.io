Last updated: February 2026

TileMelt ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes the information collected through our mobile application TileMelt (the "App") and how that information is used.

We do not require you to create an account to use the App. We do not knowingly collect direct personal information (such as your name, email address, or phone number) from you.

2. Third-Party Advertising and Analytics
We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the App. Google AdMob may collect and use data to provide personalized ads and analyze ad performance.

Data collected by Google AdMob may include:

Device Identifiers: Advertising ID (IDFA on iOS) to serve relevant ads.
Usage Data: Information about how you interact with ads (e.g., clicks, impressions) and the App.
Diagnostics: Crash logs and performance data to ensure the App works correctly.
For more information on how Google uses data, please visit Google's Privacy & Terms.

3. Data Usage
The data collected by our third-party partners is used for:

Displaying advertisements.
Analyzing ad performance.
Improving the App's stability and performance.
4. Your Choices
You can control personalized advertising settings on your iOS device:

Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking to disable tracking.
Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising to control Apple's ad targeting.
